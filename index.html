Coderwall Team Badges

a jQuery plugin that displays all Coderwall badges for your team

Usage

$('#CONTAINER').teamCoderwall({
    team: ['CODERWALL','USERNAMES','SEPARATED','BY','COMMAS'],
    badge_size: 128    // (optional) defaults to 72 & max is 200
});

Just change #CONTAINER to the ID of the DOM element where you want the badges to be displayed.

Next, you need to pass in a list of your team members' Coderwall.com usernames as an array.
